Roulette Program

You first put your starting balance and then you will choose if you will bet on odds evens, or quit.
Then you choose how much you will wager, and the bet needs to be under the amount you have.
After you choose a choice the program will genereate a number between 0-36 not including 00.
The program will let you know if you win or lose and what your remaining balance is.
the process will repeat until you quit the program.
